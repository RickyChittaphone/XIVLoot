Manage Your Raid Loot with Ease Using XIVLoot.com

Track, Assign, and Optimize Loot Distribution to Match your Own Playstyle.

Loot Tracking

XIVLoot.com offers a gear acquisition history to know exactly when someone received a new piece of gear.

Loot Distribution Score

XIVLoot.com offers a metric called PGS (Player gear score) to recommend gear distribution to the players. Every static can customize the parameters of how this score is computed to fit your own playstyle and intent in gear progression.

Gear locking

XIVLoot.com offers a way to lock players out of gear to follow your static's gear distribution rules. Manage your own rules and see when someone can and cannot receive gear.

Progression information

XIVLoot.com offers an easy way to edit a player's current and best in slot gearset all the while showing you important information like the remaining cost of your gearset. You can also painlessly import your best in slot using etro.gg.

Using with other people

XIVLoot.com is easy to use with other people. Simply share the static's link with your friends and send it to them. You can also save the static for easy access once you come back on the site.

To get started simply click on the "Create a Static" option to the left, input a name and click "Create static".
